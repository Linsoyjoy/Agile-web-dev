Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play chess?

There are great guides available online, see Lichess's beginners guide. Once you know the basics the best way to grow is by playing!

How do I track a chess game?

After logging in, navigate to your Profile page and use the "Log Game" button to record the result, opponent, and date of your match. All logged games will appear in your game history.

How is my rating calculated?

Chessmate uses the Elo rating system. Your rating updates automatically after each logged game based on the result and the rating difference between you and your opponent.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends page and search for a player by their username, or find similarly skilled players with an ELO range. Once found, click "Add Friend" to send them a friend request.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the Login page, click the "Forgot password" link and follow the instructions to reset your password via your registered email address.
